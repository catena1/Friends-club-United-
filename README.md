# Friends Club United - Official Website

Welcome to the official website of Friends Club United - More Than a Club, It's a Brotherhood!

## About Us
Friends Club United started when some friends who played street football decided to organize what they did into a football club. Our training ground is at Community Secondary School Obeama, Oyigbo Rivers State.
