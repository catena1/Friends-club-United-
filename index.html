About Friends Club United

Friends Club United was forged not in a boardroom, but on the dusty pitches of street football. Our origins are a testament to the power of camaraderie, beginning as a group of friends united by a shared love for the beautiful game.

What started as casual street football matches among companions quickly evolved into something much greater. We realized that the bond we shared on the pitch extended far beyond the boundaries of the game. We decided to organize our passion into something permanent, something that would capture the spirit of our friendship while embracing the discipline of organized football.

Today, we proudly boast a membership running into hundreds, but numbers alone don't define us. What truly makes Friends Club United special is that we are more than just a football club - we are a social brotherhood bound by unwavering commitment to one another.

Our Training Ground

You can find us training at our spiritual home: Community Secondary School Obeama, Oyigbo Rivers State. This is where dreams are nurtured and friendships are strengthened with every session.

More Than Just Football

When any fully registered member suffers misfortune, we don't just stand by and watch. We run to their intervention, offering support in whatever way we can. When our members have cause to celebrate, we join in their joy through collective contributions and shared happiness.

Our Vision

To be the foremost community-based football club, recognized not only for our excellence on the pitch but for our unwavering commitment to fostering a brotherhood that uplifts, empowers, and transforms the lives of our members and the wider community.

Supporting Pillars of Our Vision:

Sporting Excellence: Cultivate a competitive environment that nurtures local talent, promotes discipline, and achieves sustained success, inspiring the next generation of athletes in Rivers State and beyond.
Unbreakable Brotherhood: Perpetuate a self-sustaining ecosystem of mutual support where every member is a valued part of a family, ensuring that no one stands alone in times of need or celebration.
Community Anchor: Become a positive force for development, youth engagement, and social cohesion within Oyigbo and the wider Rivers State community through outreach programs and initiatives.
Legacy of Values: Build an enduring institution where the core values of Integrity, Solidarity, Passion, and Respect are passed down, ensuring the club's founding spirit thrives for generations to come.

Our Mission

To build a premier football organization rooted in excellence, brotherhood, and community impact. We achieve this through four key pillars:

Developing Talent & Character: Providing professional training focusing on technical skills, tactical understanding, physical fitness, and strong sportsmanship. We believe in building not just better players, but better individuals.
Strengthening Our Brotherhood: Actively fostering a culture of mutual care and responsibility through robust support systems, ensuring every member has a network that provides financial, emotional, and social backing in times of both challenge and triumph.
Serving Our Community: Leveraging the power of football to inspire youth, promote healthy living, and drive positive social change within Oyigbo and Rivers State through outreach programs and community initiatives.
Upholding Our Values: Operating with unwavering integrity, passion, and respect‚Äîon and off the pitch‚Äîto honor our legacy and solidify our reputation as a club that stands for more than just football.

In essence, our mission is to win matches, build men, and serve our community, all through the unifying spirit of the beautiful game.

Our Establishment

Friends Club United simply started when some friends who played street football decided to organize what they did into a football club - hence the name. Our training ground is at Community Secondary School Obeama, Oyigbo Rivers State.

We are also more or less a social brotherhood club who always run to the intervention of any fully registered member who suffers a misfortune. We run contributions for members who are celebrating too. It is the spirit of love and affection and enthusiasm shown amongst us that makes us stand up strong among other football clubs.

Our Humble Beginnings

What began as casual matches among friends has grown into a respected community institution. The same passion that fueled our street football games now drives our commitment to excellence, both on and off the pitch.
