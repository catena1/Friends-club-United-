About Friends Club United

Friends Club United was forged not in a boardroom, but on the dusty pitches of street football. Our origins are a testament to the power of camaraderie, beginning as a group of friends united by a shared love for the beautiful game.

What started as casual street football matches among companions quickly evolved into something much greater. We realized that the bond we shared on the pitch extended far beyond the boundaries of the game.

Our Vision

To be the foremost community-based football club, recognized not only for our excellence on the pitch but for our unwavering commitment to fostering a brotherhood that uplifts, empowers, and transforms the lives of our members and the wider community.

Our Mission

To build a premier football organization rooted in excellence, brotherhood, and community impact. We achieve this by developing talent, strengthening our brotherhood, serving our community, and upholding our values.
